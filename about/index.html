About Us

We work with you one-on-one for individualized college planning and application.

在孩子的成长旅途中，我们为您提供最专业最个性化的服务。

More than 20 years ago, we immigrated to the United States with a common goal for our children: to have the best education, live a prosperous life, and have an unlimited and bright future. We started getting involved in the American education system in 2004, and began college planning for our children as early as elementary school. There were no suitable counselors or programs for us at that time. Instead, we learned through books and online research; we studied cases personally by reaching out to universities, parents, teachers, and students. Through these efforts we came to intimately understand the American education system. We strive to use our experience to design personalized plans for each of our students, complementing students’ educational journeys to help them develop their academic strengths, ignite their enthusiasm for learning, and reach their true potential. These efforts not only allow students to successfully apply to prestigious universities, but also lay a solid foundation for their independent adaptation to American life. We believe with our years of expertise, our institution can help support your child’s growth for a better future in the United States.

20多年前，我们移民来了美国，虽然移民的理由每个人不一样，但都有一个共同的理想：让我们的孩子能够享受这个世界强国的教育资源，让孩子在美国生活富足，有更好的未来。我们从孩子小学起就开始关注美国的教育体系，并为孩子准备申请大学做出规划。那时市场上没有合适的询老师，我们通过网络及书本研究学习，同时与大学，家长，老师，及学生沟通学习。通过不断的努力，我们积累了丰富的经验。我们根据经验及每位学生的特点为家长孩子提供个性化的设计规划，帮助孩子发展优势，激发热情，发挥潜力。我们的努力不仅让学生成功申请到名牌大学，也为他们独立适应美国生活奠定了坚实的基础。
